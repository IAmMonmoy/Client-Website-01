Export/Import Trading Services

Hexa International is an international trading company in Bangladesh which represents overseas companies in the Bangladeshi market and promotes Bangladeshi products all over the world.

The company works as an agent and distributor of overseas products in Bangladesh and as a sourcing partner for overseas clients interested in Bangladeshi products. The Management team consists of members with extensive experiences in dealing with foreign manufacturers and buyers. The aim of the company is to create and uphold the image of Bangladesh as a growing market for overseas products and a competent supplier of quality products throughout the world.
